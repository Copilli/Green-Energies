Wind energy is a renewable energy source that harnesses the power of the wind to generate electricity. It is considered one of the most efficient and clean technologies for large-scale energy production.

How Does Wind Energy Work?

  1. Wind Capture: Wind turbines have blades that rotate with the force of the wind.
  2. Mechanical to Electrical Conversion: The motion of the blades activates a generator that converts kinetic energy into electricity.
  3. Electricity Distribution: The generated electricity is transmitted to the power grid for consumption.

Types of Wind Energy

  • Onshore Wind Energy: Wind turbines installed on land, in areas with strong wind currents.
  • Offshore Wind Energy: Wind turbines installed in the sea, where winds are stronger and more constant.
